    Our story

    Welcome to Elgo Glamour World, your premier destination for beauty and rejuvenation. Since our establishment, we have been dedicated to enhancing the natural beauty and confidence of our clients through exceptional salon services. Our story is one of passion, creativity, and a commitment to delivering the highest standard of care.It all began with a vision—a vision to create a space where individuals could escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and indulge in a personalized beauty experience. Kripa Mitra, the visionary behind Elgo Glamour World, drew upon years of industry expertise and a deep love for all things beauty to bring this dream to life.

    About Elgo Glamour World

    In 2012, Elgo Glamour World opened its doors, marking the beginning of an extraordinary journey. From day one, our mission has been to create an oasis where our clients can relax, unwind, and feel truly pampered. We believe that beauty is not just about the outer appearance but also about nurturing the inner self. With this philosophy in mind, we carefully curate a range of services that cater to your unique needs.

    Our Mission

    We do only what we are great on.Our mission is to inspire you to embrace your creativity and experiment with different styles, colors, and trends. We are committed to providing exceptional salon services in a welcoming and uplifting environment. We view beauty as a powerful form of self-expression.

    Our Goals

    Our primary goal is to exceed the expectations of our clients and provide unparalleled satisfaction. We strive to create a welcoming and nurturing environment where every client feels valued, listened to, and pampered. Your happiness and delight are at the core of everything we do.We understand that each individual has unique beauty needs and preferences. Our goal is to provide personalized consultations and tailored services to every client.

    Expertise

    Our team consists of skilled professionals with years of experience and a deep understanding of the beauty industry. You can rely on their expertise to deliver exceptional results.

    Client Satisfaction

    Our ultimate goal is your satisfaction. We take the time to listen to your desires, provide personalized consultations, and tailor our services to meet your specific requirements. Your happiness is our top priority.
